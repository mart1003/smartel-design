Isometric
Illustration


This piece showcases isometric illustration techniques. The illustration depicts the evolution of music players over the last few decades. I started with sketches on paper, which then turned into flat digital images. I used illustrator to give the items depth. I wanted to give the illustration a unique and stylized colour scheme.
